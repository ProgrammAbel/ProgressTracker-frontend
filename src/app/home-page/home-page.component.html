How it works:

  1. Create an account and log in
  2. Select your subjects
  3. Tick any topics that you have completed in class
  4. Select a confidence level for each topic
  5. When you want to revise:
    • Press the checkbox at the top to sort by priority
    • This orders the topics based on what you need to revise most
    • Pick the topic at the top of the list, and revise it
    • When you are finished, update your confidence level and set your last review date to the day you revised
